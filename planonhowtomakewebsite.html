Steps oh how to build a website: 1. Start by defining the goal of your website clearly. 2. Carry out a visual research by visiting other websites. 3. Prepare the text to use before start building the website. 4. Prepare a detailed site map and that includes all the pages we plan to create and how they all connect to each other. 5. Prepare a website name and that is a combination of your business name and an extension the website a unique online address. 6. Decide the website layout. Decide whether you are creating it from scratch or from professional and well-equipped templates. 7. Decide on the colours to use on your website. 8. Select the right fonts. 9. Make content more visual and digestible. 10. Add social bar to website 11. Optimize for SEO 12. Make the website mobile friendly. 13. Ask people you trust for a second opinion on the website.
